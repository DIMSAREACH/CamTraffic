# Equipment Preparation Checklist (Task 130)

## Purpose

Confirm all required collection equipment is prepared before field data acquisition.

## Required equipment

- Smartphone (1080p+ camera, enough free storage)
- Dashcam (1080p+, stable mounting)
- Extra memory card (high-speed, formatted, tested)
- Power bank (fully charged, cable verified)
- Car or motorcycle (road-ready, legal operation documents available)
- Laptop (for daily backup and quality checks)
- External hard drive (primary backup destination)

## Pre-departure checks

- Charge smartphone, dashcam battery packs, and power bank.
- Set consistent time/date on all recording devices.
- Verify video/photo settings (resolution, FPS, format).
- Confirm memory card write/read test passed.
- Prepare camera mount and cleaning cloth for lens maintenance.

## End-of-day workflow

- Copy all captured media to laptop and external hard drive.
- Verify sample playback for data integrity.
- Update collection log and source metadata manifests.
- Flag low-quality captures for recollection plan.

## Status

Task 130 checklist is considered complete once all required equipment is available and
pre-departure checks pass in at least one dry-run session.
