# Collection Location Plan (Task 132)

## Goal

Plan a balanced capture map across high-density urban roads, national highways, and
key provinces to improve generalization for detection and ANPR.

## Phnom Penh priority corridors

- Monivong Blvd
- Russian Blvd
- Mao Tse Toung Blvd
- Norodom Blvd
- Hun Sen Blvd

## National roads

- NR1
- NR4
- NR5
- NR6

## Province targets

- Siem Reap
- Battambang
- Kampot
- Sihanoukville

## Coverage strategy

- Capture both peak and off-peak traffic windows.
- Include daytime, dusk, nighttime, and rainy sessions.
- Collect from both moving dashcam and stationary roadside viewpoints.
- Ensure representative coverage for motorcycles, private cars, buses, trucks, and taxis.
