# Chapter 7 - Conclusion and Future Work (Final)

Task: 397
Date: 2026-07-10

## 7.1 Conclusion

CamTraffic demonstrates a complete AI-assisted traffic enforcement platform for Cambodian operational context. The project integrates computer vision detection, OCR, backend business workflows, role-based portals, and deployment automation into a single auditable system.

## 7.2 Major Outcomes

- End-to-end pipeline from camera evidence to enforcement workflow
- Reproducible deployment stack with operations runbooks
- Multi-role portals supporting administration, officer review, and driver interaction
- Documented evaluation and validation artifacts across AI, integration, QA, and deployment

## 7.3 Limitations

- Large-scale GPU production benchmarking is environment-dependent
- Khmer-script OCR remains a future enhancement area
- National-level live integrations are outside current scope

## 7.4 Future Work

1. Expand production-grade GPU training and inferencing infrastructure.
2. Improve OCR robustness with curated local plate datasets and verification loops.
3. Add mobile-first officer workflows and field evidence capture.
4. Integrate with external registry/government data sources where legally allowed.
5. Extend multi-camera monitoring and advanced analytics dashboards.

## 7.5 Final Statement

The project has reached thesis-ready completion with full documentation and deliverable traceability, establishing a strong foundation for pilot deployment and further academic or operational extensions.
